About

I have a soft spot for biophysics and am optimistic that we will someday discover a “grand unified theory of human disease biology” — a combination of physical and computational frameworks to explain the macro and microscale organization of the body.

When not thinking about the aforementioned subjects, I amuse myself with reading and conversations. If you have any recommendations for thought-provoking blogs or novels, please reach out — I’d be happy to trade!
